Welcome to the WitherBot Docs

WitherBot has a Cash System allowing you to get richer and become the richest on the server or the most respected.

WitherBot is an excellent moderation bot for all needs. Multiple commands such as kick, ban, mute are available at your disposition. WitherBot automatically unmutes users after their mute is over, AND will prevent mute evasion (users leaving and rejoining to lose their Muted role).

If you find any bugs/exploits or issues please report them!
